Rules:
First, press the "Get a category" button to produce your first category. To the left you will see a letter and to the right will be the category itself. Each person must write down an answer starting with the letter given, making sure no-one else sees it. Repeat this for all 26 letters of the alphabet. Once all 26 have had a category, go through the categories again and award pointas follows: - 3 point if the answer is correct and unique - 1 point if the answer is correct but isn't unique The person with the most points wins! Solo Mode (alternate game mode!) Try to get through 26 of the categories without googling it. Its surprisingly hard when you get to the later letters!
